About Demeter Events

Demeter Events offers full-service event planning for special occasions in the Bay Area and beyond.

Our mission is to help our clients create events that align with their values. We've developed a deep knowledge base and formed partnerships to make it easy and budget-friendly to support people and businesses that promote sustainable practices and ensure your event is meaningfully unique.

We offer transparent, fairly-priced services that enable each of our clients to achieve their vision while supporting local businesses and eco-friendly or progressive enterprises.

Debra Kao, Founder and Lead Planner

Debra started Demeter Events after planning her own wedding and realizing two things. First, brides or grooms should never have to plan a wedding alone. Second, a special event becomes a deeply sincere one when you can support people and businesses that align with their values. After learning the ins-and-outs of how to green every element of her wedding, she decided to start Demeter Events so that she could share what she's learned.

Debra has worked in the tech sector as a change management specialist, an agile scrum master, and a project manager. She's also a burner who holds the ten principles close. She loves amplifying human potential, eliminating waste, and finds endless satisfaction in ensuring objects are used to their full potential.

Our Services

We offer an array of services to help you celebrate with family and friends.

Wedding Planning

From end-to-end event design to day-of coordination, we take your vision and make it into a reality. We're here to make wedding planning stress-free, while ensuring the big day runs smoothly for you and your guests.

Corporate Retreats

Bonding with your coworkers takes so much much more than a happy hour. Let us create a unique experience that has your team learning from one another and deepening working relationships.

Travel

We'll do the research so that every minute you spend travelling is spent on doing what you want to do. Planning a trip doesn't have to drain your time - tell us what you're envisioning and we will make it happen.

Pricing

Every couple and every wedding is different, which is why we offer flexible wedding packages to suit your needs. We'd love to schedule an complimentary initial consultation to discuss - please send us a quick note here.

We believe in transparency and offer competitve pricing with the top planners in the Bay Area. Coordination services start at local benchmarks: day-of for $1000, partial planning for $5,000, and full planning for $10,000. We're happy to talk further about our pricing philosophy.
